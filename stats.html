Bear Stats

Bears pretty much do as they please and they live all over the world. Here are some neato bear stats:

    Scientific name: Ursidae
    Speed: 25 mph (Polar Bear)
    Lifespan: 20 years (Giant Panda)
    Mass: 150-220 lbs (Giant panda), 990 lbs (Polar Bear), 240 lbs (American black bear), 180-1,300 lbs (Brown Bear)
    Did you know: There is some debate about the largest bear in the world
